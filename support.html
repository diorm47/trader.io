What are the benefits of the dual-token system?

  • Traders Token (TDT): This token allows you to participate in governance decisions, gives you access to special trading discounts, and a share of the platform’s profits.
  • Secure Trade Token (STT): Primarily used for transaction fees, STT enhances transaction security and allows for transparency in every trade.
  • Enhanced Security and Participation: This system not only increases platform security but also engages users in the ecosystem, promoting a community-driven governance model.
